Roll for the Great Old Ones

Game Description

Roll for Great Old Ones is a unique game in which players draft dice
out of a general pool to program actions on their investigator board. Thus leaving
behind "cultist dice", which will trigger actions during the scenario.
The investigators have to negotiate which dice to use and which to leave
behind, knowing quite well what might happen.
